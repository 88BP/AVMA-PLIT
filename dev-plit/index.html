our brand story
For over 50 years, AVMA PLIT has been the trusted leader in the veterinary community, providing a comprehensive insurance program designed for veterinarians by veterinarians. In 2014, we embarked on a comprehensive research study to ensure we continue to deliver our leading edge veterinary insurance program and are always evolving with the current and future needs of the veterinary community.
We interviewed over 1,500 current, former and potential AVMA PLIT participants and practice managers and listened to their thoughts on how they viewed our insurance program. Everything we learned from them culminated in the new AVMA PLIT: Protecting you through it all initiative you see here today. And it’s more than simply a new look. This exciting new initiative will ultimately enhance AVMA members’ understanding of the insurance solutions and resources available to them through comprehensive tools and educational programs that help veterinarians minimize their risk of facing claims and help them better understand their business needs.

“For more than 50 years we’ve helped protect the reputations of veterinarians at all stages of their careers and helped guide them through tough legal situations and license complaints. AVMA members continue to tell us that the PLIT program is one of the most valuable components of their AVMA membership; we want to build on our success and ensure that our colleagues are aware of the full scope of products and services—from professional liability to business and personal insurance.”

Janet Donlin, DVM, AVMA PLIT CEO

research findings
The opinions of the veterinary community were instrumental in guiding us toward how we can serve AVMA members even better. What we heard again and again from the veterinarians we spoke with was they deemed it “absolutely essential” that they participate in a program that is overseen by veterinarians, tailored to the needs of veterinarians and veterinary practices.
Other key findings include:
  • The AVMA PLIT insurance program is viewed as a top benefit of AVMA membership. More than 60,000 AVMA members are PLIT program policyholders.
  • About one in four respondents admit they do not fully understand their malpractice policy.
  • Nearly all respondents are aware that the PLIT program gives them access to malpractice and license defense coverage, more than one-third (34%) are not certain that PLIT program provided business property and general liability insurance, and homeowners insurance (26%).
  • 80 percent of respondents indicate that it is essential that veterinarians oversee their insurance program.
our brand promise
After we completed our research study, we synthesized the new learnings and refined our strategic platform. This process ultimately led us toward enhancing our brand promise and further strengthened how we communicate our mission to every veterinarian.
Once we distilled everything we learned from the veterinary community, we then crafted our new brand promise. This message will serve as the foundation for guiding all our actions and activities, as well as those of our strategic partners, for the entire veterinary community.
The AVMA PLIT is your trusted professional insurance partner, delivering the most complete and supportive program available to veterinarians, so you can always be confident you have everything you need to remain protected.
developing the new AVMA PLIT look
After we finalized our new brand promise, we partnered with a branding agency to create our refreshed look. Over a few months, they created a range of engaging and visually diverse concepts that brought our updated brand promise to life. We then presented those concepts to a focus group of veterinarians to learn which concepts best visualized how they viewed the AVMA PLIT program. Their feedback ultimately shaped the finalized look and tone of our refreshed brand, including everything from the tagline to the logo choice, the color palette and the presentation of information. Here’s a highlight of what they had to say:
Other key findings include:
  • Overall, veterinarians preferred images that showed veterinarians interacting with animals because they convey real and relatable situations.
  • The new logo rose to the top because AVMA and PLIT are of equal size, reflecting an equal partnership, with the bold treatment promoting PLIT while demonstrating that AVMA is an integral part of the package.
  • Many preferred the “Protecting you through it all” tagline because it reassures them that they can call PLIT for help and work with veterinarians to solve problems.
  • The phrases and headlines were felt to be very meaningful and believable and did the best job of conveying AVMA PLIT’s expertise and specialization.
  • The color palette elicited a more relaxed and comfortable response that ensures AVMA PLIT continues to feel approachable and valued.
  • The newly stylized treatment of information, which now includes highlight points, short lists and concise executive summaries, makes it even easier to digest insurance information.

“This is an exciting time for the PLIT as we continue to provide protection for a very diverse group of professionals ranging from equine and food animal veterinarians to companion animal, exotic, and wildlife veterinarians. Whether you’re working in a private practice, corporate general practice, specialty clinic or academic setting or if you are an associate or an owner, your colleagues at the PLIT are here to protect you through it all.”

Andrew R. Clark, DVM, MBA, AVMA PLIT Chair
